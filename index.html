Who We Are

The Netherlands Catchball Association

The Netherlands Catchball Association (NCA) is the official governing body for women's catchball in the Netherlands. Founded in December 2019, we connect women through sport, competition, and community — building a league that grows stronger every season.

The Sport

What is Catchball?

Catchball is a dynamic women's team sport combining volleyball and handball. Two teams of 6 compete across a net — passing, catching, no spiking needed. Accessible, competitive, and seriously fun.

🎯 🎯

Easy to Learn

Simple rules make catchball perfect for beginners and experienced athletes alike.

🤝 🤝

Team Sport

6 players per side — built on communication, teamwork, and trust.

💪 💪

For Every Woman

No age limit, no prior experience needed. Just bring your energy!

🌟 🌟

Social & Fun

Make lasting friendships on and off the court.
